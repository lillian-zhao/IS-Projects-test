Community Partner Requirements

As a Community Partner, these are the most important conditions we request from you:
  • Attend the following mandatory meetings:
    • Client Orientation Meeting on CMU Campus
    • Final Presentations Orientation Meeting on CMU Campus
  • Provide biweekly feedback on the team and the project progress via a short online form and periodic conversations with the supervising faculty member.
  • Invest your time - there is no financial cost to be a community partner.

Detailed Requirements for Each Course

67-373: Information Systems Consulting Project Course (Spring)

As a 67-373 Community Partner, your responsibilities are to:

  • Attend the mandatory Client Orientation Meeting on CMU Campus on Wednesday, January 31, 2024, 11:00am - 1:10pm.
    • Your presence at this meeting is critical the a good start with the program. Please indicate in the comments box below if you have a constraint on this day.
  • Meet and work with the student development team each and every week for 1 hour (this may include time with additional key staff in your organization depending on tasks).
  • Read interim project reports prepared by the team and provide them with feedback within 2 days.
  • Provide biweekly brief feedback on the team and the project progress via a short online form and periodic conversations with the supervising faculty member.
  • Read the final report, providing feedback to the student team within 2 days, and submit Feedback Form to the faculty instructor giving approval to publish the report.
  • Make a required brief presentation with the student team at the end of the semester at the Community Technology Forum at the Carnegie Mellon University campus on Friday, May 3, 2024 from 12:30pm - 4:30pm and stay for the entire session to hear about and celebrate all partnership presentations.
  • Your presence at this full session is critical to the program.

67-240: Mobile Web Design & Development Course (Fall)

As a 67-240 Community Partner, your responsibilities are to:

  • Attend the mandatory Client Orientation Meeting on CMU Campus on Wednesday, October 25, 2024, 11:00am - 1:10pm.
    • Your presence at this meeting is critical the a good start with the program. Please indicate in the comments box below if you have a constraint on this day.
  • Meet and work with three student development teams each and every week for 1 hour (this may include time with additional key staff in your organization depending on tasks).
    • You will be working with these three teams to make multiple versions of the website.
  • Read interim project reports prepared by the team and provide them with feedback within 2 days.
  • Attend a required brief presentation with the student team at the end of the semester at the Community Technology Forum at the Carnegie Mellon University campus on Friday, December 7, 2024 from 12:30pm - 4:30pm and stay for the entire session to hear about and celebrate all partnership presentations.
