Could you use a fresh set of eyes on your organization processes? 

Become an integral part of our Tartan community and connect with talented student teams through Carnegie Mellon University’s Information Systems courses!

Join Us!

Since 2015, the Information Systems course here at Carnegie Mellon University has partnered with community partners to create sustainable and applicable information systems solutions that seek to bridge the digital divide.

Join us as a community partner to work on exciting projects and tap into top-notch student consultations. Our students love applying their classroom know-how to solve real-world problems. Successful outcomes have included making organizational process pipelines 90% faster and simplifying database tracking for clients. Work with our student teams to find technology solutions that directly impact your community challenges.
